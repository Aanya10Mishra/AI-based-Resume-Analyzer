# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

Our community is dedicated to providing an inclusive and harassment-free experience. All contributors are expected to uphold this Code of Conduct.

Standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Use welcoming language and be considerate of differing viewpoints.
- Do not post or tolerate harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue titled "CODE OF CONDUCT REPORT" or contact the maintainers directly.

Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content or restrict contributors who violate the Code of Conduct.

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
